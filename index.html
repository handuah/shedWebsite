AUTHENTIC COWORKING SPACES

Welcome to Workshed. We are more than just an office space; we are a dynamic coworking community of startup founders, freelancers, entrepreneurs and small teams who are working to build dreams. Coworking is the use of an office or other working environment by people who are self-employed or working for different employers, typically so as to share equipment, ideas, and knowledge - harnessing the powers of share-economy. The whole idea of coworking is to bring bright, creative people together and let the ideas collide. Great ideas are colliding at Workshed.

FEATURES & BENEFITS

Excellent Location

Located in the bustling Baatsona quarter of the Spintex Road, we have created a serene working environment, offering you a perfect blend. Carefully embedded in the midst of commercial and residential establishments, our building is conveniently located close to several points of interest.

Spacious Workspace

Surround yourself with hardworking professionals like yourself in our spacious shared working space. Our current location has a floor space of 220 square meters and comfortably sits up to forty people. If you need to have some privacy, we got you covered, you can book our meeting rooms on an hourly basis.

Dedicated Wi-Fi

Having reliable internet connection is no longer a luxury, it is a necessity. Experience fast and reliable WiFi that would keep you connected and allow you to get your work done. As a Workshed member, you get a dedicated WiFi service because your work and its success are important to us.

Coffee Bar

"Coffee", they say, "is the common man's gold." Inspired by this, we just had to throw in a little bit of caffeine-induced luxury. Need a little break? Hang out at our coffee bar and enjoy freshly brewed coffee, hot chocolate, fruit juices and other mouthwatering snacks at unbeatable prices.

All The Day Access

We are open and ready for you to work from 8:00 am to 5:30 pm Monday through Friday. Occasionally, on days we do not have after-hour events scheduled, the space would be open till 8:00pm. Prior notice would be given to members informing them about work-late days.

Free Event Pass

Get discounted access to workshops, hangouts, lectures, training relevant to growing your business and entrepreneurial know-how.

Happy Hour

A casual atmosphere allows people to loosen up and interact informally, resulting in an alternative mode of knowledge transfer. The after-work happy-hour events at Workshed are designed for de-stressing and promoting coworker interaction. Relax, network and catch some fun at our after-work cocktails and networking events.

Incredible Networking

As an entrepreneur, networking is a critical key to your growth. Be part of a community, expand your professional and personal network, share ideas and collaborate on projects too large to handle all alone. We provide a community where you can establish relationships beneficial for your growth.
